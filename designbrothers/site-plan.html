Rates

Build a website for you: My starting rate for building a website is $1500 which includes a homepage, and a child page. From there you can choose how you would like your pages to be styled. The price will go up depending on how many child pages you want.

Time Frame: In a hurry, and need your site put on a rush? We can make you our utmost priority, and finish your website within the needed time frame. We only charge an extra fee of $500.

Competitive rates: Here at Design brothers we believe in quality over the price you are going to pay. For a low starting rate of $1500 that you will not find anywhere else locally. Our rates are very competitive and reasonable compared to other web designers in the area.
